Cloudiness

From the data it is observable that there are high levels of cloudiness across varying latitudes indicating there is not a strong correlation between closeness to the Equator and cloudiness percentage. The highest cloudiness percentage of 100% were recorded for cities between the latitidues of -20 and 40. The varriation in cloudiness percentage is understandable as cloudiness varies based on the hour of day, time of year and seasonality.
